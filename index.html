BIOGRAPHY

A singer-songwriter born and raised on the island paradise of Bohol, Philippines. Vocalist of the band Chill Blends & FTW.

With the acoustic guitar as his go-to instrument, he serenades us with mellow tunes and pumps up the energy with alternative rock music. A man of few words, he prefers to express strong emotions & voice out his opinion on pressing issues through songwriting. He pursues to sing about unpopular, taboo themes that demand a voice. He is a Himo'g Huni Ika-4 na Hugna Finalist with his song entry "Bakod".
